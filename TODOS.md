## P2 — Pricing test infrastructure
**What:** After first 10 Sprint customers, test $499 vs $599 vs $699 pricing.
**Why:** $499 may be underpriced for the buyer profile (exec/business owner who spends $5K on a bike). Even a 20% price increase = 20% more revenue at no extra cost.
**How:** Create a second Stripe Payment Link at the test price. Alternate the link in /sprint for a defined test period (2 weeks per price point, min 10 customers per point for statistical signal).
**Effort:** XS (human: 2h / CC: 5 min)
**Depends on:** First 10 paying Sprint customers
