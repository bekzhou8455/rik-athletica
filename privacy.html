Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026  ·  RIK Athletic Nutrition Inc.

Short version: We collect only what we need to run your protocol and ship your order. We never sell your personal information. Period.

1. Who We Are

RIK Athletic Nutrition Inc. ("RIK", "we", "us", or "our") operates the website at rikathletica.com. Our registered address is 477 Madison Avenue, New York City, NY 10022. You can reach us at bek.zhou@rikathletica.com for any privacy-related questions.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, and mailing address when you make a purchase or complete our intake form.
  • Sprint intake data: When you complete the Sprint Protocol intake form, you provide health-adjacent information including gastrointestinal history, training schedule and weekly training hours, body weight, estimated sweat rate, target race details (distance, date, course), and dietary restrictions or food allergies. This is sensitive health-adjacent data and is treated with strict confidentiality.
  • Calculator inputs: If you use our IRONMAN Nutrition Calculator, the inputs you enter (race distance, training pace, GI history) are processed in your browser and not stored on our servers unless you provide your email address.
  • Email address (optional): If you provide your email through the calculator, we store it to send you your results and relevant nutrition information. You can unsubscribe at any time.

We also collect certain information automatically:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, referring URL, browser type, and device type via Google Analytics 4 (GA4).
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics (GA4) and affiliate tracking (Rewardful). See Section 9 for our full cookie policy. No advertising cookies are set by us.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your personalized fueling protocol based on your intake data (Sprint customers)
  • To select and customize product recommendations for your race
  • To conduct weekly protocol revisions and adjustments throughout your Sprint program
  • To process your order and fulfill shipment through our third-party logistics provider
  • To send order confirmations, shipping notifications, and protocol delivery emails
  • To send nutrition tips, race preparation content, and product updates (you can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time)
  • To improve the calculator and our product recommendations

We may use anonymized and aggregated intake data for internal research and development purposes, including improving our protocol engine and fueling algorithms. Anonymized data cannot be traced back to any individual athlete.

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment card details on our servers. When you click a payment button, you are directed to a Stripe-hosted checkout page. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We share data only with the following service providers, solely as necessary to deliver our services:

  • ShipWizard (3PL fulfillment): We share your name and shipping address with ShipWizard to fulfill and ship your order. ShipWizard does not use your data for any purpose other than order fulfillment.
  • Stripe (payment processing): Payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe for transaction processing. We do not have access to your full card details.
  • Dropbox Sign: Sprint customers sign a service agreement via Dropbox Sign. Your name and email are shared with Dropbox Sign to facilitate electronic signatures.

No intake data, health-adjacent data, or protocol information is shared with any third party.

6. Affiliate Tracking

If you arrive at our site via an affiliate link (e.g., a link shared by a coach or training partner), we use Rewardful to set a tracking cookie. This cookie records which affiliate referred you and is used to calculate affiliate commissions. It does not track your browsing activity outside of our site.

7. Health and Nutrition Data

The Sprint intake form collects sensitive health-adjacent information including gastrointestinal history, body weight, sweat rate, dietary restrictions, and training data. This information is used exclusively to produce your personalized fueling protocol and to make weekly revisions throughout your program. It is handled with strict confidentiality and is not shared with any third party except as described in this policy.

This is not medical data as defined under HIPAA. RIK protocols are sports nutrition guidance, not medical advice. RIK is not a registered dietitian or licensed healthcare provider.

8. Data Retention

We retain your intake and protocol data for the duration of your Sprint program plus 12 months following the completion of your program. After this retention period, your data is deleted from our active systems unless you request earlier deletion or unless retention is required by law.

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing bek.zhou@rikathletica.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following cookies and tracking technologies on our site:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): We use GA4 to collect anonymized usage data including pages visited, session duration, traffic sources, and device information. GA4 data is used to understand how visitors use our site and to improve the user experience. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Rewardful (affiliate tracking): A first-party cookie is set when you arrive via an affiliate link. This cookie identifies the referring affiliate and is used to calculate commissions. It does not track activity outside our site.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or cross-site tracking technologies.

  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete the personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions permitted by law.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal information. If this practice ever changes, we will provide a clear opt-out mechanism.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at bek.zhou@rikathletica.com. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days as required by law.
